Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our long-term chauffeur assignments in Texas.

What is the minimum duration for a long-term assignment?

We typically consider "long-term" to reflect assignments valid for 2 weeks or greater, but flexible terms are available.

Will I have the same driver every day?

Yes, priority #1 is continuity. We aim to assign a single primary chauffeur, with a dedicated backup for off-days, ensuring familiarity.

Can I travel between cities?

Yes. Your dedicated vehicle and driver can take you between Houston, Dallas, San Antonio, and Austin as needed.
