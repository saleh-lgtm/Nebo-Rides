Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about group airport shuttles.

Where will the bus meet us?

Due to airport regulations, buses stage in commercial lots. Our greeters will meet you at baggage claim and guide you to the loading zone.

How much luggage can we bring?

Our motorcoaches feature large underbelly bays perfect for heavy luggage, trade show materials, and equipment.

What happens if a flight is delayed?

We track all flights. For small delays, we may hold the shuttle; for major delays, we can dispatch a relief vehicle.

Ready to move your group?

Our logistics team is standing by to plan your airport transfers.
