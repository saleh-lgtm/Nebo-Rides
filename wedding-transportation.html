Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about wedding transportation.

Can we decorate the car?

A tasteful 'Just Married' signage is usually allowed if it doesn't damage the paint. Please confirm specific decorations with us beforehand.

Is there a minimum rental time?

Yes, wedding packages typically have a 3-4 hour minimum due to the 'split' nature of the ceremony and reception.

Do you provide champagne?

We provide ice and glasses. Due to liquor licensing, clients usually supply the specific alcohol they prefer.
