Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about conference and gala logistics.

How far in advance should we book conference transport?

For large events, we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance to secure sufficient vehicle inventory.

Do you provide on-site coordinators?

Yes, for complex manifests, we provide on-site ground marshals to manage driver communication and passenger loading.

Can we brand the shuttle buses?

Absolutely. We offer custom vinyl wrapping and magnetic logos to turn the fleet into mobile billboards for your event.
