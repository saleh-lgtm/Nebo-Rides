Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about manifest management.

How do you group passengers?

We analyze arrival times and group attendees within set windows (e.g., 30 minutes) to maximize shuttle efficiency.

What is the deadline for manifest changes?

We accept changes up to 24 hours prior for optimal planning, but have 'sweeper' vehicles for last-minute additions.

Is attendee data secure?

Yes, we are fully GDPR compliant and transmit all manifests via secure, encrypted channels.
