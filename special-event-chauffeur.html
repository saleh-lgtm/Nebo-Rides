Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about special event chauffeur services.

Why is hourly booking required for special events?

Hourly booking ensures your vehicle is dedicated to you for the entire evening, allowing for indefinite end times and immediate availability.

Can we customize the vehicle?

Yes, we can remove branding or add specific flags/ribbons for diplomatic or themed events upon request.

What amenities are included?

Vehicles come stocked with water and chargers. Champagne and other specific amenities can be arranged upon request.
