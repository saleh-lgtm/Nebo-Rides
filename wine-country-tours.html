Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our wine country tours.

Are tasting fees included?

Hourly rates cover the vehicle and chauffeur. Tasting fees are generally paid directly to the winery.

Can we eat in the car?

Light snacks are permitted to keep you fueled. We can also arrange picnic stops or restaurant reservations.

How many wineries should we visit?

We recommend visiting no more than 3-4 wineries per day to allow for a relaxed pace and avoid palate fatigue.

Ready to taste the world?

Our team will curate the perfect vineyard itinerary for your group.
