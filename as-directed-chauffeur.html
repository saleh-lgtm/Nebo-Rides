Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our as-directed hourly chauffeur service.

What is the difference between hourly and point-to-point service?

Hourly service provides a dedicated vehicle and chauffeur for a set period with unlimited stops, whereas point-to-point is a direct transfer from A to B.

How are overages billed for hourly trips?

Time extending beyond your initial booking is typically billed in 15-minute increments at the hourly rate.

Is there a mileage limit for hourly service?

Hourly service generally includes travel within the greater metro area. Long-distance inter-city trips may incur additional mileage fees.
