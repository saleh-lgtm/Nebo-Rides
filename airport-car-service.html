Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our airport car service.

Is a car service better than Uber for the airport?

Yes. Unlike rideshare, Nebo Rides offers guaranteed pricing, real-time flight tracking, and vetted professional chauffeurs. We eliminate the risk of "no-shows," surge pricing, and the uncertainty of wondering if your driver will actually be there when you land.

How does flight tracking work?

We sync your reservation with your airline's tail number data. If your flight is late or early, your chauffeur automatically adjusts, and you are never charged for wait time caused by the airline. This FAA-integrated system tracks the actual aircraft, not just the flight number.

Can I book a car service for an early morning flight?

Absolutely. Our 24/7 dispatch desk ensures that 3 AM or 4 AM pickups are staged 15 minutes early, providing peace of mind. We understand that early departures require absolute reliability, which is why we never cancel and always confirm the night before.

What airports do you service?

We provide primary coverage across the Texas Triangle (AUS, DFW, DAL, IAH, HOU, SAT) with local dispatch teams, plus nationwide service in 50+ markets including NYC, Miami, Chicago, and Los Angeles through our vetted affiliate network.

Do you offer corporate accounts?

Yes. We offer comprehensive corporate accounts with centralized billing, dedicated account managers, priority dispatch, and real-time GPS tracking for duty of care compliance. Contact us to set up your corporate account.
