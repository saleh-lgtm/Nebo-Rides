Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our corporate chauffeur service.

Can I request the same chauffeur for every trip?

Yes. For our corporate chauffeur programs, we prioritize consistency, allowing you to build a relationship with a dedicated driver who understands your routes, timing preferences, and professional requirements.

How does "as-directed" service work?

As-directed service means the vehicle and chauffeur are at your disposal for a set period. You can make as many stops as needed or change your destination on the fly without needing to book new trips. It's the ultimate flexibility for executives with dynamic schedules.

Are your chauffeurs trained for security?

All corporate chauffeurs undergo basic executive protection training, focusing on situational awareness, defensive driving, and total discretion. For enhanced security requirements, we can coordinate with your existing protection detail.

Can I make confidential calls in the vehicle?

Absolutely. Our chauffeurs operate under strict NDA-compliant confidentiality protocols. They are trained to provide a secure, private environment for sensitive business discussions. Privacy partitions are available on request in select vehicles.

How do I book a corporate chauffeur?

Corporate chauffeur service can be booked through our online form, by calling our 24/7 dispatch line, or directly through your dedicated account manager. For regular service, we recommend setting up a corporate account for streamlined booking and billing.
