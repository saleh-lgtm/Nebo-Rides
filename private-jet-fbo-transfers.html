Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our private jet and FBO transfer services.

Can Nebo Rides track my private jet for ground pickup?

Yes. We monitor tail numbers in real-time and integrate directly with FBO dispatchers to ensure your chauffeur is on the ramp or curbside precisely when you land. This independent tracking means we see diversions and delays before the FBO desk updates.

Do you provide tarmac or ramp access at FBOs?

Where permitted by FBO policy, we provide immediate tarmac access for direct aircraft-to-vehicle transfers, coordinating all security clearances in advance. This is available at most Signature, Atlantic, and Million Air locations.

Are your chauffeurs trained in executive protection?

All FBO-designated chauffeurs are vetted and trained in basic executive protection protocols, prioritizing discretion, security-conscious positioning, and confidentiality. For enhanced security requirements, we can coordinate with your existing protection detail.

What FBOs do you service?

We service all major FBO chains including Signature Flight Support, Atlantic Aviation, Million Air, and independent operators globally. Primary coverage includes Texas (AUS, ADS, SGR), Northeast (TEB, HPN, BED), Southeast (OPF, FXE, PDK), and West Coast (VNY, SNA, BFI).

Can you handle last-minute manifest changes?

Absolutely. Our 24/7 operations team is equipped to handle manifest changes, passenger count adjustments, and schedule modifications in real-time. We maintain backup vehicle staging for high-priority accounts to ensure flexibility.
