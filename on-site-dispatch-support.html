Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about on-site dispatch support.

How many coordinators do I need?

We generally recommend one coordinator for every 50 guests or 3 simultaneous vehicle departures to ensure smooth operations.

What do the coordinators wear?

Our staff wears professional "Event Black" attire or can wear your branded polo shirts upon request.

Do you offer airport greeters?

Yes, we provide both airport greeters (meet & greet) and on-site dispatchers (hotel/venue management).
