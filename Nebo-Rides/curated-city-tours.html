Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our private city tours.

Is the driver a licensed tour guide?

Our chauffeurs are knowledgeable locals, but for deep historical context, we recommend hiring a separate Blue Badge guide which we can arrange.

Are attraction tickets included?

No, entry tickets are not included. We recommend purchasing them in advance to skip lines.

Can we customize the itinerary?

Absolutely. Send us your 'must-see' list and we will build the most efficient route around your preferences.
