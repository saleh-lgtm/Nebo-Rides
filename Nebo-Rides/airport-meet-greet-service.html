Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Meet & Greet service.

Where exactly will my chauffeur meet me?

Your professional chauffeur will meet you inside the terminal at baggage claim or immediately after customs exit, holding a personalized sign with your name or company logo.

What if my flight is delayed?

We track your flight in real-time using automated systems. If you are delayed, your chauffeur's arrival time automatically adjusts at no additional charge. You'll never be left waiting.

Can you accommodate corporate branding?

Absolutely. We offer digital signage that can display your company logo, and our corporate accounts include branded communications for all passenger touchpoints.

How much wait time is included?

Every Meet & Greet service includes 30 minutes of complimentary wait time from your scheduled pickup. For international flights, we include 60 minutes to account for customs and immigration processing.

What's your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup. Changes can be made anytime through our app, by phone, or via email at no extra charge.

Still have questions?

Our team is available 24/7 to assist with your booking.
