EXPERTISE

Legal Compliance

We understand the customs declarations, transport regulations, and export compliance requirements involved in moving high-value goods across state and national borders. Our logistics team coordinates with customs brokers and border authorities in advance to ensure your jewelry, art, or prototype shipments clear checkpoints without delay. This legal compliance expertise is essential for secure asset transport operations that cross jurisdictional boundaries, particularly for auction houses and galleries moving pieces internationally.

TRUSTWORTHINESS

Double Authentication

Every pickup and delivery requires two-factor authentication combining government-issued photo ID verification with a unique digital token generated for each transport assignment. This double-authentication protocol ensures the asset is released only to the authorized recipient, creating an unbreakable chain of custody from origin to destination. Every handoff is timestamped, geotagged, and photographically documented, providing a complete audit trail that satisfies insurance underwriters and legal compliance requirements.

AUTHORITATIVENESS

Insurance

We offer specific cargo insurance riders tailored to the declared value of your assets, with coverage options ranging from standard commercial liability to specialized fine art and jewelry transit policies. Items valued above $10,000 require a current independent appraisal prior to transport. Our insurance partners are experienced in covering high-value asset movements for auction houses, private collectors, and luxury brands, ensuring your items are fully protected from pickup to delivery.

Chain of Custody

Every asset transfer follows a documented chain-of-custody protocol with two-factor authentication at both pickup and delivery points. Real-Time Tracking

Our client portal provides real-time GPS tracking of your asset's location down to the meter throughout the entire transport journey. You receive automated status updates at key checkpoints including departure confirmation, route progress alerts, and delivery completion notifications. This continuous visibility gives asset owners, insurers, and security teams the confidence that comes from knowing exactly where high-value items are at every moment during transit.

Discreet Transport

Unlike branded armored trucks that advertise high-value cargo, our discreet approach relies on obscurity as the first layer of security. This philosophy is particularly valued by jewelry dealers, private collectors, and fashion houses transporting valuable items between showrooms, vaults, and events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about luxury asset protection.

Do you offer on-board couriers?

Yes, for high-value air transport we provide On-Board Courier (OBC) service where a trained staff member physically accompanies the asset in the aircraft cabin throughout the flight. The courier maintains continuous chain of custody from pickup through check-in, boarding, landing, and final delivery to the recipient. This service is frequently used by jewelry dealers, auction houses, and luxury brands transporting items that are too valuable or too time-sensitive for traditional cargo shipping methods.

What items do you NOT transport?

We strictly do not transport illegal contraband, hazardous materials, firearms, or undeclared currency and financial instruments. All items must be legally owned, properly documented, and declared with accurate valuations for insurance purposes. Our intake process includes a compliance review to verify that every transport meets applicable federal, state, and international regulations before we accept the assignment.

How is insurance handled?

We offer specific cargo insurance riders tailored to the declared value of each asset being transported, with coverage options from our specialized fine art and jewelry transit insurance partners. Assets valued over $10,000 generally require a current independent appraisal to activate coverage. Our logistics team will work with your insurance broker to ensure coverage terms align with your existing policies and that all documentation meets underwriter requirements.

Can you transport large art pieces or entire collections?

Yes, our climate-controlled Sprinter vans accommodate large framed artwork, sculpture, and multi-piece collections with custom padding and suspension-dampened cargo areas. For gallery exhibitions and auction house consignments, we coordinate multi-vehicle operations to move entire collections between venues safely. Our art transport logistics team has experience with major galleries in New York, London, and Geneva, and can coordinate with professional art handlers for loading and unloading at both ends.

Is armed escort available for high-value movements?

In jurisdictions where permitted, we deploy off-duty law enforcement officers as armed escorts for exceptionally high-value asset movements. Armed escort availability depends on local regulations and typically requires 48 to 72 hours advance notice for proper credentialing and route planning. Our security team will assess your specific transport requirements and recommend the appropriate level of protection based on asset value, route risk profile, and applicable jurisdictional laws.
