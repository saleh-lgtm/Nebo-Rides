Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about luxury asset protection.

Do you offer on-board couriers?

Yes, for high-value air transport, we provide 'On-Board Courier' service where a staff member flies with the asset in the cabin.

What items do you NOT transport?

We strictly do not transport illegal contraband, hazardous materials, or undeclared currency/instruments.

How is insurance handled?

We offer specific cargo insurance riders for high-value items. Assets valued over $10k generally require a current appraisal.
