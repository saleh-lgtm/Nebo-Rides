Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our employee transportation service.

What is the minimum number of employees required?

Our employee transportation programs are designed for flexibility. While we can accommodate groups as small as 10 employees with our SUV fleet, our shuttle programs become most cost-effective at 25+ employees per route. We'll work with your HR team to design the most efficient solution for your workforce size.

How do you handle multiple shift schedules?

We specialize in multi-shift operations. Our dispatch team coordinates separate pickup schedules for morning, swing, and night shifts. Routes are optimized for each shift window, and we provide 24/7 service availability for late-night and early-morning schedules common in manufacturing, healthcare, and hospitality.

Can employees track their shuttle in real-time?

Yes. All shuttles are GPS-tracked, and employees can view real-time arrival estimates through our mobile app or SMS alerts. This reduces wait-time anxiety and helps employees plan their morning routine around accurate pickup times.

What happens if an employee misses their scheduled pickup?

We maintain a 2-minute grace period at each stop. If an employee misses their shuttle, they can request on-demand backup service (if included in your program) or wait for the next scheduled route. Our app allows employees to mark themselves as "not riding" in advance, which optimizes the route for remaining passengers.

How is billing handled for employee transportation?

We offer flexible billing models: monthly flat-rate contracts for fixed routes, per-ride billing for on-demand service, or hybrid arrangements. All invoices include detailed ridership reports, cost-per-employee analytics, and can be allocated to specific cost centers or departments.
