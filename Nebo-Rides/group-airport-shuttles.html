Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about group airport shuttles.

Where will the bus meet us?

Due to airport regulations, buses stage in commercial lots. Our greeters will meet you at baggage claim and guide you to the loading zone. At most major airports, the shuttle pulls up to the designated commercial vehicle area within minutes of your group being fully assembled.

How much luggage can we bring?

Our motorcoaches feature large underbelly bays perfect for heavy luggage, trade show materials, and equipment. Each full-size motorcoach accommodates approximately 56 full-size suitcases in under-floor compartments, and our greeters assist with loading to ensure nothing is left behind.

What happens if a flight is delayed?

We track all flights in real time. For small delays, we may hold the shuttle; for major delays, we can dispatch a relief vehicle for the delayed subgroup. Our dispatch center monitors every incoming flight so your event logistics stay on schedule regardless of airline disruptions.

Can we book multiple shuttles for staggered arrivals?

Absolutely. Staggered arrivals are our specialty. We build a shuttle schedule aligned with your flight manifest, dispatching vehicles in waves as groups arrive. Each wave has its own greeter and headcount verification, so no one waits longer than necessary at the terminal.

Do you provide on-board Wi-Fi and refreshments?

Full-size motorcoaches come equipped with Wi-Fi, a PA system, and restrooms. We can also arrange bottled water, branded signage, and welcome packets upon request. For premium corporate events, we offer upgraded hospitality packages including snacks and beverage service on board.
