Yes, our drivers are fully trained in procession etiquette, including headlight use and hazard light protocols.

Can you access the gravesite?

Our vehicles can drive on paved cemetery paths to bring elderly or mobility-impaired guests directly to the gravesite.

We understand the urgency of these events and can often mobilize our fleet with less than 24 hours notice.
