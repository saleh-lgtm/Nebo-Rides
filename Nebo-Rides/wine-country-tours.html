Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our wine country tours.

Are tasting fees included?

Hourly rates cover the vehicle and chauffeur. Tasting fees are generally paid directly to the winery. Most Napa Valley tasting fees range from $40-$100 per person, while European wine regions often include complimentary tastings with a purchase. Our concierge team can provide estimated tasting costs for your specific itinerary during the planning phase so you can budget the full experience accurately.

Can we eat in the car?

Light snacks are permitted to keep you fueled. We can also arrange picnic stops or restaurant reservations. Many of our wine country clients enjoy a gourmet picnic at a scenic vineyard overlook midway through the tour, which our concierge team can arrange with local catering partners. For those preferring a sit-down experience, we secure reservations at vineyard restaurants like The French Laundry, Bistro Jeanty, or Osteria Francescana depending on your wine region.

How many wineries should we visit?

We recommend visiting no more than 3-4 wineries per day to allow for a relaxed pace and avoid palate fatigue. This optimal pacing includes travel time between stops, a lunch break, and sufficient time at each tasting room to fully appreciate the wines without feeling rushed. For full-day tours of 8+ hours, some groups add a fifth stop at a more casual tasting bar for a lighter final experience.

Can you arrange private or barrel room tastings?

Yes, our concierge team has established relationships with winemakers and tasting room managers across all major wine regions. We can arrange private tastings, barrel room experiences, cave tours, and meet-the-winemaker sessions at both renowned estates and hidden boutique producers. These exclusive experiences are often unavailable to walk-in visitors or group tour buses, giving our private tour clients access to a truly premium level of wine country hospitality.

What happens if someone in our group has too much to drink?

This is precisely why private chauffeur service is the safest way to tour wine country. Your professional driver remains sober and attentive throughout the entire day, providing a safe, comfortable vehicle for anyone who needs to rest between stops. We carry water, snacks, and comfort items as standard equipment. If a guest needs to return to the hotel early, we can adjust the itinerary flexibly or coordinate a second vehicle for the remaining group to continue their tour.
