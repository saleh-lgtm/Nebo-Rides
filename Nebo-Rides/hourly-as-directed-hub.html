Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our hourly and as-directed chauffeur service.

What happens if I go over time?

It's simple. You are billed for the additional time in 15 or 30-minute increments at the agreed hourly rate. There is no penalty for extending, and your driver will remain with you as long as needed.

Can we leave the city?

Generally, hourly service is for metro usage. Long-distance trips are usually billed as point-to-point transfers. Your quote will clearly specify the included service radius, so there are never surprise charges.

Is gratuity included?

Our quotes are all-inclusive. Gratuity, fuel, and taxes are typically included in the hourly rate. You will see a single, transparent price with no hidden fees or surcharges at the end of your service.

Ready to reserve your block?

Our dispatch team is standing by to coordinate your hourly service.
