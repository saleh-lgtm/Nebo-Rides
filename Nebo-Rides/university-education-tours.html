Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our university tour transportation.

Is luggage safe in the car?

Absolutely. Your luggage remains locked and secure in the vehicle with the driver standing by while you are on your walking tour, attending information sessions, or meeting with admissions staff. This is particularly valuable on multi-day college tours where you check out of one hotel in the morning and visit two or three campuses before checking into the next hotel that evening. Think of the vehicle as your secure mobile base throughout the entire tour.

Can the driver suggest lunch spots?

Yes, our drivers know the local student hangouts and can recommend authentic spots where students actually eat and socialize, giving your family a true feel for campus life beyond the official tour. Whether it is the best pizza place near Brown or the student coffee shop that overlooks the Stanford quad, these recommendations help prospective students visualize their daily life at each school. Drivers can also point out off-campus neighborhoods, nearby amenities, and safety considerations.

Do you help plan the route?

We offer complimentary itinerary review to ensure your travel times between campuses are realistic and stress-free. Our planning team analyzes the driving distances, typical traffic patterns, and campus tour schedules to flag any conflicts before your trip begins. We will suggest optimal departure times, recommend which schools to group together geographically, and build in buffer time so an extended campus walk never derails the rest of your day.

Can my child tour colleges without a parent present?

Yes. We regularly transport unaccompanied minors on college tours with enhanced safety protocols. Every chauffeur assigned to minor transport undergoes additional background screening and carries verifiable identification. Parents receive a real-time GPS tracking link and automatic notifications at each pickup, campus arrival, and drop-off. A dedicated dispatcher monitors the trip from start to finish, and your child has a direct line to our 24/7 support team at all times.

How many campuses can we realistically visit in one day?

With a private chauffeur, most families can comfortably visit 2 to 3 campuses in a single day, depending on the driving distances between schools and the length of each campus tour. In dense academic corridors like the Boston area (Harvard, MIT, Tufts) or the Research Triangle (Duke, UNC, NC State), three visits are very achievable. We build itineraries that balance thorough campus exploration with realistic transit times so you never feel rushed.
