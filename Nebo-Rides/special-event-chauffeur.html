Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about special event chauffeur services.

Why is hourly booking required for special events?

Hourly booking ensures your vehicle is dedicated to you for the entire evening, allowing for indefinite end times and immediate availability. Special events like premieres, galas, and award shows rarely follow a fixed schedule, so hourly service guarantees your chauffeur is on standby regardless of how long the evening runs. This approach also means no surge pricing or availability gaps between the event and after-party.

Can we customize the vehicle?

Yes, we can remove branding or add specific flags/ribbons for diplomatic or themed events upon request. Vehicle customization options include branded signage, color-coordinated ribbon accents, and interior upgrades such as custom floral arrangements. We recommend submitting customization requests at least 72 hours before the event to ensure flawless execution.

What amenities are included?

Vehicles come stocked with water and chargers. Champagne and other specific amenities can be arranged upon request. Our concierge team can also coordinate custom additions such as branded gift bags, specific floral arrangements, or particular beverage selections to match your event theme and personal preferences.

How far in advance should I book special event transportation?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for standard events and 2-3 months ahead for major occasions like fashion weeks, film festivals, or award shows. During peak event seasons in cities like New York, Los Angeles, and Cannes, premium vehicles and experienced event chauffeurs are in extremely high demand, and early reservations guarantee the specific vehicle class you require.

Can you coordinate transportation for multiple guests at one event?

Absolutely. We specialize in multi-vehicle event logistics, managing fleets of sedans, SUVs, and Sprinters for large-scale occasions like galas, product launches, and corporate premieres. Our event operations team creates a detailed manifest with staggered arrival times, designated pick-up and drop-off zones, and real-time dispatch coordination to ensure every guest arrives seamlessly.
