We always have a backup vehicle on standby. Mechanical issues are rare, but on your wedding day, we take zero risks. Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about wedding transportation.

Can we decorate the car?

A tasteful 'Just Married' signage is usually allowed if it doesn't damage the paint. Please confirm specific decorations with us beforehand. We welcome ribbon accents, magnetic signs, and floral arrangements that can be attached without adhesive or suction cups. For custom decoration requests, our wedding coordination team will review and approve specifics at least one week before the ceremony to ensure your vision is executed without any risk of vehicle damage.

Is there a minimum rental time?

Yes, wedding packages typically have a 3-4 hour minimum due to the 'split' nature of the ceremony and reception. This minimum covers the bridal pickup, ceremony arrival, photo session transport, and reception delivery with adequate buffer time between each stage. For all-day wedding coverage including the rehearsal dinner and grand exit, we offer extended packages of 8-12 hours at preferential hourly rates.

Do you provide champagne?

We provide ice and glasses. Due to liquor licensing, clients usually supply the specific alcohol they prefer. Our vehicles come equipped with chilled champagne flutes, ice buckets, and napkins so the couple can toast immediately after the ceremony. If you have specific beverage preferences or dietary requirements, let us know during the planning phase and we will ensure everything is ready for your celebration.

How many guests can your shuttle service accommodate?

Our wedding shuttle service scales to accommodate weddings of any size. A single luxury coach bus handles up to 56 guests per loop, and we run continuous shuttles between the hotel and venue to move larger groups efficiently. For weddings with 100-300 guests, we deploy multiple coaches on staggered schedules, ensuring no guest waits more than 15-20 minutes for the next pickup. Your wedding coordinator will design the shuttle schedule based on your guest count and venue logistics.

Do you handle destination weddings outside the US?

Absolutely. Destination weddings are one of our specialties, with established operations in Lake Como, Tuscany, the French Riviera, Santorini, and dozens of other popular wedding destinations worldwide. Our global network provides locally sourced luxury vehicles with English-speaking chauffeurs, venue-specific logistics planning, and multi-day transportation packages covering everything from airport arrivals through the farewell brunch. We coordinate all ground logistics so your wedding planner can focus on the celebration itself.
