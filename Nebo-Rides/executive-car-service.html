Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our executive car service.

What is the difference between an executive car service and a standard car service?

Executive car service prioritizes vetted professional chauffeurs, late-model luxury vehicles, and advanced corporate protocols like duty of care, discretion, and mobile office amenities that standard services lack. Our chauffeurs undergo specialized training in executive etiquette, security awareness, and defensive driving.

Does the car service include Wi-Fi for working on the go?

Yes. All Nebo Rides executive sedans and SUVs are equipped with complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi and power outlets to ensure productivity remains uninterrupted. Our vehicles serve as true mobile offices where you can take calls, respond to emails, and prepare for meetings.

Can I set up a corporate account for my company?

Absolutely. We offer specialized corporate account programs that include centralized billing, priority dispatch, and dedicated account management for frequent business travelers. Corporate accounts provide consolidated monthly invoicing, cost center allocation, and comprehensive reporting for finance teams.

How far in advance should I book executive car service?

For guaranteed vehicle selection and premium sedan availability, we recommend booking 24-48 hours in advance. However, corporate account holders receive priority dispatch and we can often accommodate same-day requests. For multi-vehicle bookings or events, we suggest 1-2 weeks advance notice.

Are your chauffeurs trained in executive protection?

Yes. All chauffeurs complete training in situational awareness, defensive driving, and discretion protocols. They understand information security and maintain strict confidentiality. For enhanced security requirements, we can coordinate with your existing protection detail or provide additional vetted resources.
