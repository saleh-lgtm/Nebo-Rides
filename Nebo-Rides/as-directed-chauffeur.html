Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our as-directed hourly chauffeur service.

What is the difference between hourly and point-to-point service?

Hourly service provides a dedicated vehicle and chauffeur for a set period with unlimited stops, whereas point-to-point is a direct transfer from A to B.

How are overages billed for hourly trips?

Time extending beyond your initial booking is typically billed in 15-minute increments at the hourly rate.

Is there a mileage limit for hourly service?

Hourly service generally includes travel within the greater metro area. Long-distance inter-city trips may incur additional mileage fees. Your quote will clearly specify the included service radius, so there are never surprise charges.

Can I extend my hourly booking if I need more time?

Absolutely. Simply notify your chauffeur or our dispatch team, and we will extend your reservation in real-time. Extensions are billed in 15-minute increments at your contracted hourly rate. There is no penalty for extending, and your driver will remain with you as long as needed — subject to DOT rest regulations for bookings exceeding 12 hours.

Do you offer corporate accounts for recurring hourly service?

Yes. We offer comprehensive corporate accounts with centralized billing, dedicated account managers, priority dispatch, and real-time GPS tracking for duty of care compliance. Corporate clients booking recurring hourly service also benefit from preferred pricing tiers and guaranteed vehicle availability during peak demand periods.
