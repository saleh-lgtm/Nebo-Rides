Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about sporting event transportation.

Can we tailgate in the vehicle?

Yes, Sprinters are ideal for tailgating. However, glass bottles and grills must adhere to the specific stadium's regulations. Our Sprinter limos feature standing headroom, climate control, and sound systems that make them the ultimate pre-game gathering spot. We advise confirming your stadium's specific tailgating policies with us during booking so we can ensure full compliance.

How much time should we allow for leaving?

We recommend booking at least 60-90 minutes of post-game time, as exiting stadium lots can take significant time. Major venues like MetLife Stadium and Wembley can experience 30-60 minute pedestrian clearing periods before vehicles can move. Building this buffer into your hourly booking ensures your chauffeur is waiting when you arrive at the pre-arranged meeting point rather than rushing to reach you.

Is alcohol allowed in the vehicle?

Alcohol is generally permitted in partitioned vehicles (Limousines/Sprinters) but check local laws for sedans/SUVs. Most jurisdictions allow open containers in the passenger compartment of chauffeured vehicles with a physical partition. We strongly encourage the use of our professional chauffeur service as a safe alternative to driving after game-day celebrations where alcohol is consumed.

Can you transport large groups to a game?

Absolutely. We offer Mercedes Sprinter limos for groups up to 14 and luxury coach buses for fan clubs and corporate hospitality groups of 50 or more. Multi-vehicle coordination is our specialty, and we can manage pickup from multiple hotels, staggered arrivals, and synchronized post-game departures for groups of any size attending the same sporting event.

Do you cover international sporting events like the World Cup or Olympics?

Yes, Nebo Rides provides sporting event transportation for major international tournaments including the FIFA World Cup, Olympic Games, and Formula 1 Grand Prix races worldwide. Our global partner network secures local vehicles, security credentials, and event-specific transportation permits in every host city. We begin planning logistics months before the tournament to ensure seamless coverage across multiple venues and match days.
