Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about sporting event transportation.

Can we tailgate in the vehicle?

Yes, Sprinters are ideal for tailgating. However, glass bottles and grills must adhere to the specific stadium's regulations.

How much time should we allow for leaving?

We recommend booking at least 60-90 minutes of post-game time, as exiting stadium lots can take significant time.

Is alcohol allowed in the vehicle?

Alcohol is generally permitted in partitioned vehicles (Limousines/Sprinters) but check local laws for sedans/SUVs.
