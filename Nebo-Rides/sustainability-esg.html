Green Logistics

Sustainable operations at every stage of your journey.

EV Priority

Dispatching electric vehicles (EQS, i7) whenever available in your service area. Our EV-first dispatch algorithm automatically assigns zero-emission vehicles as the default option for all corporate accounts with sustainability mandates. When a fully electric vehicle is unavailable, the system escalates to plug-in hybrids before considering conventional luxury sedans, ensuring maximum emission reduction across your travel program.

Carbon Reporting

Providing detailed CO2 emission data for corporate ESG auditing and Scope 3 reporting requirements. Every ride generates a carbon footprint calculation using EPA and DEFRA emission factors, with data exportable in CSV and PDF formats for integration into your sustainability management platform. Our reporting accuracy meets the standards required by GRI, CDP, and TCFD disclosure frameworks.

Certified Offsets

Neutralizing unavoidable emissions through verified carbon offset programs certified by Gold Standard and Verra. For rides where electric or hybrid vehicles are unavailable, we automatically calculate and retire carbon credits equal to the journey's CO2 output. This carbon-neutral guarantee ensures your ground transportation achieves net-zero emissions regardless of vehicle type or destination.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our sustainable transportation services.

Is there an extra cost for EVs?

No, we price our Electric Vehicle class competitively with standard luxury sedans to encourage sustainable adoption. Our goal is to eliminate cost as a barrier to choosing eco-friendly transportation, making it easy for corporate travel managers to default to EV-first policies. In many markets, our EV chauffeur rates are actually lower than traditional luxury sedan pricing due to reduced fuel and maintenance costs.

What if an EV isn't available?

In markets where EV inventory is sold out, we prioritize high-efficiency hybrids to minimize environmental impact. Our dispatch system follows a strict green hierarchy: full EV first, plug-in hybrid second, and conventional luxury sedan only as a last resort. Any ride completed in a combustion vehicle is automatically enrolled in our certified carbon offset program to maintain your organization's net-zero ground transportation commitment.

How do you calculate emissions?

We use recognized standards (EPA/DEFRA) based on vehicle type and distance traveled to provide audit-ready carbon data. Each ride's emission calculation factors in the specific vehicle model, fuel type, distance, and regional electricity grid mix for EVs. This granular methodology ensures your Scope 3 transportation data meets the verification requirements of sustainability auditors and ESG rating agencies.

Can the carbon data integrate with our existing sustainability platform?

Yes, our carbon emission data is available via API and standard export formats (CSV, PDF) for seamless integration with platforms like Salesforce Net Zero Cloud, Watershed, and Persefoni. Corporate accounts receive quarterly sustainability reports that align with GRI, CDP, and TCFD disclosure frameworks, making it easy for your sustainability team to incorporate ground transportation data into annual ESG filings.

Which cities have the best EV availability?

Our strongest EV fleets are deployed in London, Amsterdam, Paris, San Francisco, and Los Angeles, where mature charging infrastructure supports reliable all-electric service. These cities also operate Ultra Low Emission Zones (ULEZ) where our EV fleet provides unrestricted access that conventional vehicles cannot match. We are rapidly expanding EV availability to New York, Dubai, and Singapore, with new zero-emission vehicles added monthly.
