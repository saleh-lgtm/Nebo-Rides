Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our flight tracking and delay protection.

How do you track my flight?

We use tail number data from FAA-authorized radar streams. This is more accurate than airline apps or website trackers because we're tracking the actual aircraft, not just the flight number. This allows us to see diversions, gate changes, and taxi delays before they appear on standard airport monitors.

What happens if my flight is cancelled?

Contact our dispatch desk immediately. We will put your reservation on hold and reschedule for your new arrival time without applying cancellation penalties. Your reservation stays active until you land, regardless of how many times your flight is rescheduled.

Do I have to call you if my flight is late?

No. Our system monitors everything automatically. Your chauffeur will be informed of the new arrival time without any action required from you. You'll receive an automated SMS update when we detect a schedule change, and again when your chauffeur is dispatched.

Are there any fees if my flight is significantly delayed?

No. Our Zero Wait Fees policy means you're never charged extra for airline delays. Whether your flight is delayed by 1 hour or 10 hours, the price you were quoted is the price you pay. This is built into our business model.

How early does my chauffeur arrive at the airport?

Your chauffeur is dispatched based on "Wheels Down" time, not scheduled arrival. This means they're positioned at the terminal precisely when you deplane, not waiting for hours due to early dispatch. For international arrivals, we add buffer time for customs processing.
