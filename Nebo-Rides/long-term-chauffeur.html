Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our long-term chauffeur contracts.

Can I drive the vehicle myself?

No, our long-term chauffeur services include a professional driver at all times. We do not offer self-drive rentals. If you prefer to drive your own vehicle on certain days, we offer a flexible "Driver Only" option where our chauffeur drives your personal car when you need them and stands down when you don't.

What happens if I don't get along with my driver?

We offer a no-questions-asked replacement policy to ensure you are perfectly matched with your chauffeur. Simply notify your dedicated account manager, and we will assign a new driver within 24 hours. Our matching process improves with feedback, and most clients find their ideal fit within the first two assignments.

Is fuel included in the monthly rate?

Typically, fuel is billed as an additional expense or included in a flat day rate, depending on the specific contract terms. We offer both all-inclusive packages where fuel, tolls, and parking are bundled into a single monthly fee, as well as itemized billing where each expense is reported separately for maximum transparency.

Can I leave personal items in the vehicle between trips?

Yes, one of the key benefits of a long-term contract is that the vehicle is dedicated to you for the duration of your agreement. You can leave car seats, umbrellas, chargers, and personal belongings in the vehicle between trips, treating it as your own. The vehicle is securely stored overnight at our facility or in a location you designate.

What is the minimum contract duration?

Our long-term contracts start at a minimum of one week, with the most significant volume discounts available for monthly and quarterly commitments. We offer flexible terms that can be extended or shortened with reasonable notice, making our contracts ideal for seasonal residences, corporate relocations, and project-based assignments where the timeline may shift.
