Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pet transportation.

Can my pet travel alone?

Yes, we can transport pets solo (e.g., to the groomer) as long as you load them and an authorized person receives them at the destination.

Do you take large dogs?

Absolutely. Our SUVs are perfect for Great Danes, Mastiffs, and other large breeds that don't fit in standard sedans.

Is there a cleaning fee?

We expect some shedding and do not charge for it. A cleaning fee is only applied if a pet has a significant accident requiring professional shampooing.

Our pet transport team is standing by for your companion's next journey.
