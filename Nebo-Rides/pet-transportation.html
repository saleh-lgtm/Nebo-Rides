An anxious pet is a distracted driver. We keep it calm.

EXPERTISE

Pet CPR

Select drivers are certified in pet first-aid and CPR, prepared for emergencies during transport. This includes recognizing signs of heatstroke, choking, and anxiety attacks in both dogs and cats. Our pet transport specialists undergo annual recertification to maintain the highest standards of animal safety.

TRUSTWORTHINESS

Pit Stops

On long trips, we schedule regular potty and water breaks at safe rest areas, keeping owners updated with photos. Each rest stop is pre-scouted for pet-friendly facilities including grassy areas and shade. We send real-time GPS tracking and photo updates so you always know exactly where your companion is during transit.

AUTHORITATIVENESS

USDA Compliance

For interstate commercial transport of animals, we adhere to USDA transport regulations regarding temperature and space. Our vehicles maintain cabin temperatures between 45-85 degrees Fahrenheit as required by federal animal welfare standards. All long-distance pet relocation services include documentation that meets USDA Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service requirements.

Animal Logistics

Every comfort measure for your companion's journey.

Cabin Travel

Pets ride in the main cabin, not cargo, secured with seatbelt harnesses or in approved carriers placed on the seat. Our climate-controlled interiors maintain optimal temperatures for animal comfort, and heavy-duty seat covers protect the vehicle while providing a cozy surface for your pet.

Potty Breaks

On long-distance pet transport trips, we schedule regular hydration and relief stops at pre-vetted, pet-safe rest areas. Each break is documented with photos sent directly to the pet owner, and our drivers carry fresh water, travel bowls, and waste bags as standard equipment.

Sanitization

Every vehicle undergoes thorough sanitization between pet transport rides using veterinary-grade cleaning products. This heavy-duty protocol eliminates allergens, dander, and odors so the next passenger, whether human or animal, enters a fresh, hygienic cabin environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pet transportation.

Can my pet travel alone?

Yes, we can transport pets solo (e.g., to the groomer) as long as you load them and an authorized person receives them at the destination. Our drivers are experienced in handling animals independently and will send you photo updates throughout the journey. This unaccompanied pet taxi service is one of our most popular offerings for busy pet owners.

Do you take large dogs?

Absolutely. Our SUVs are perfect for Great Danes, Mastiffs, and other large breeds that don't fit in standard sedans. We recommend the Cadillac Escalade or Sprinter Van for extra-large breeds, as these vehicles provide ample floor space and high ceilings for maximum comfort. Simply mention your dog's breed and weight when booking so we can assign the ideal vehicle.

Is there a cleaning fee?

We expect some shedding and do not charge for it. A cleaning fee is only applied if a pet has a significant accident requiring professional shampooing. Our vehicles come equipped with heavy-duty seat covers and protective liners as standard for all pet transport bookings, so normal wear and tear is already accounted for in our pricing.

What types of animals do you transport?

While dogs and cats make up the majority of our pet taxi bookings, we also transport rabbits, birds in secure cages, and other small domestic animals. Exotic pets or animals requiring specialized containment should be discussed with our dispatch team in advance. We will match the right vehicle and driver to your specific animal's needs.

How far in advance should I book for long-distance pet relocation?

For cross-country pet relocation, we recommend booking at least two weeks in advance to secure your preferred vehicle and schedule optimal rest stops along the route. This lead time also allows us to coordinate any necessary USDA health certificates or interstate travel documentation your pet may require. For local vet visits and grooming appointments, same-day bookings are often available.
