Executive Summary: The Companion

Respecting the rhythm of our elders.

Patience

No Rush. We understand that entering and exiting the vehicle takes time. Our drivers never rush a senior passenger.

Assistance

Door-Through-Door. We carry luggage, fold walkers, and offer a steady arm from the front door to the car seat.

Communication

Family Updates. We text the adult children upon pickup and safe drop-off, providing peace of mind to the whole family.

Relocation

Long Haul. Moving to a warmer climate? We drive seniors to Florida or Arizona in comfort, avoiding the stress of airports.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our senior transportation service.

Will the driver help with luggage?

Yes, the chauffeur handles all lifting and loading of luggage, walkers, and personal items. The passenger lifts nothing.

Can pets come along?

Yes, for relocation trips, pets are welcome to travel in the cabin with their owner. Please notify us when booking.

My parent struggles with technology.

Our drivers are happy to assist with setting the climate control, radio, or even helping dial a phone call to you.

Need senior transport?

Our caring team is ready to coordinate your senior transportation.
