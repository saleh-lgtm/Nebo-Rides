Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Greenside Protocol.

Can you get tarmac access at every airport?

Tarmac access is subject to local regulations and airport security (MARSEC) levels. We will confirm availability for your specific location.

Why do you need the tail number?

The tail number allows us to track the specific aircraft's movement and is required by FBOs to grant ramp access for the vehicle.

Do I still need to show ID?

Yes, even with Greenside service, customs and immigration agents may need to verify passports or ID at the aircraft steps.
