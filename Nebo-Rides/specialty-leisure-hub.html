Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our specialty and leisure chauffeur services.

Can we bring food?

Yes. Snacks and drinks are welcome in our leisure vehicles.

Are tours guided?

Our drivers are knowledgeable, but they are not licensed tour guides. For deep history, we recommend hiring a guide to ride along.

Is there a cleaning fee?

Generally no, unless there is a significant mess (e.g., sickness or spill).
