Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our event and group transportation services.

How far in advance should I book?

For large buses, we recommend 3-6 months. For sedans, 24 hours is usually sufficient. The earlier you book, the better we can secure your preferred vehicle types and coordinate with venues on staging logistics.

Can we drink alcohol on the bus?

In most jurisdictions, yes, provided all passengers are of legal age. A deposit may be required. We will confirm the specific regulations for your event location during the planning process.

Do you provide coordinators?

Yes. For events with more than 3 vehicles, we highly recommend adding an on-site coordinator to manage the flow. Our coordinators handle loading zones, passenger counts, and real-time communication with dispatch.
