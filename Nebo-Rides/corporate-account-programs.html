Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our corporate account programs.

How do I set up a corporate car service account?

Setting up an account is simple. Complete our online request form, and an account manager will contact you to finalize your billing preferences, user list, and service requirements. Most accounts are fully operational within 24-48 hours.

Does a corporate account offer priority service?

Yes. Corporate account holders receive priority dispatch status, ensuring higher vehicle availability even during high-demand events and peak travel periods. Your reservations are flagged for priority fulfillment.

Can I manage multiple travelers under one account?

Absolutely. Our corporate portal allows you to add multiple bookers and travelers, with options for department-level billing and reporting. Each traveler can have their own profile with saved preferences.

What billing options are available?

We offer flexible billing to match your accounting needs: credit card on file for real-time charges, per-trip billing for individual expense tracking, or consolidated monthly invoicing for simplified AP processing. Your account manager can configure the optimal structure.

Is there a minimum commitment or setup fee?

No. There are no setup fees and no minimum monthly spend required. You simply pay for the rides you use at our transparent corporate rates. The account benefits—priority dispatch, dedicated manager, unified billing—are included at no additional cost.
