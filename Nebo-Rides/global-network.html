Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our global chauffeur network.

What currency is used for billing?

We bill all rides in your home currency (USD, GBP, EUR) to eliminate foreign transaction fees and exchange rate confusion. Your consolidated monthly invoice includes itemized trip details with cost-center codes, making reconciliation straightforward for your finance team. This centralized billing approach is one of the primary reasons corporate travel managers choose our global chauffeur network over local providers.

Do international drivers speak English?

While many local drivers speak English, we provide 24/7 US-based English-speaking support to bridge any communication gaps. Your travelers can call our dispatch center at any hour from any country to resolve routing questions, relay special instructions, or handle last-minute changes. We also send driver details and vehicle information in English prior to every pickup so your team always knows exactly who is arriving.

How do you vet international partners?

We rigorously audit all affiliates, checking fleet age, insurance coverage, driver background checks, and historical service performance. Unlike lead-generation platforms, we are a managed global network that holds every partner to measurable quality standards. Affiliates that fall below our benchmarks are removed immediately, and every ride generates a quality score that feeds into our continuous improvement system.

How far in advance should I book international rides?

We recommend booking international chauffeur services at least 24 to 48 hours in advance to guarantee vehicle availability, particularly during major conferences and events. However, corporate account holders with priority dispatch can often secure same-day bookings in most major cities. Our 24/7 operations team is always available to accommodate urgent travel needs across all time zones.

Can I set up a corporate account for my entire organization?

Absolutely. Our corporate accounts are designed for organizations of any size, from boutique firms to Fortune 500 enterprises. You receive a dedicated account manager, customizable travel policies, department-level cost-center tagging, and a centralized booking portal that your entire team can use. Setup typically takes less than 48 hours from initial contact to first ride.
