Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our global chauffeur network.

What currency is used for billing?

We bill all rides in your home currency (USD, GBP, EUR) to eliminate foreign transaction fees and exchange rate confusion.

Do international drivers speak English?

While many local drivers speak English, we provide 24/7 US-based English-speaking support to bridge any communication gaps.

How do you vet international partners?

We rigorously audit all affiliates, checking fleet age, insurance, and service history. We are a managed network, not a lead generator.
