The Guardian Protocol: Engineering Safety

Trust is earned every single mile.

EXPERTISE

Car Seat Pro

Our drivers are trained and certified in the proper installation of LATCH car seats, rear-facing infant carriers, and booster seats for every age group. We can provide age-appropriate child safety seats or securely install your own before departure. Every nanny driver completes a child passenger safety course that meets national highway safety standards, ensuring your child rides in full compliance with local car seat laws.

TRUSTWORTHINESS

Code Word

For extra security, we establish a secret "Code Word" that the child asks for before entering the vehicle, ensuring no unauthorized person can pick up your child. This verification protocol is shared exclusively between the parent, driver, and child at the time of booking. Our child transportation security measures go beyond what any rideshare platform offers, giving families complete peace of mind during every school run and activity pickup.

AUTHORITATIVENESS

School Authorization

We work directly with school administrators to ensure our nanny drivers are listed as authorized guardians for pickup and drop-off lines. Our team handles all required school transportation authorization forms, ID verification, and carpool lane credentials before the first ride. This proactive coordination with school staff eliminates delays and ensures a seamless, stress-free experience every morning and afternoon.

Minor Logistics

Every safeguard in place for your child's journey.

Live Tracking

A live GPS tracking link is sent directly to the parent's phone the moment the vehicle departs with your child. You can monitor the entire route in real-time, from pickup to school drop-off, with estimated arrival times updated every 30 seconds. This transparent child transportation tracking system ensures you always know exactly where your child is during every school run.

Door-to-Door Handoff

Our nanny drivers never leave a child unattended at the curb. They wait until the child has physically entered the school building or has been received by a designated adult guardian. For custody exchange situations, we follow strict handoff protocols that comply with court-ordered visitation schedules and provide timestamped confirmation to both parents.

Driver Continuity

Children thrive on consistency, which is why we assign the same dedicated nanny driver for all recurring school runs and activity pickups. Your child will recognize and trust their driver, eliminating the anxiety that comes with a stranger at the wheel. This continuity also means the driver learns your child's routine, preferred routes, and any special needs or preferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about nanny and minor transportation.

What makes a driver safe for my child?

Our nanny drivers undergo enhanced background checks including Vulnerable Sector clearance, multi-jurisdictional criminal screening, and verified clean driving records. Every driver also completes child passenger safety training and is recertified annually. Unlike rideshare platforms that randomly assign drivers, Nebo Rides hand-selects each nanny chauffeur specifically for child transportation assignments.

Can I track the ride?

Yes, you receive a live GPS tracking link the moment the vehicle departs with your child, allowing you to monitor the entire journey in real-time from your phone or computer. The tracking updates every 30 seconds with estimated arrival times, and you receive automatic text notifications at pickup and drop-off. This real-time visibility gives parents complete peace of mind during every school run.

Do you provide car seats?

We provide age-appropriate car seats including rear-facing infant carriers, forward-facing toddler seats, and booster seats for older children upon request. Alternatively, our drivers are trained to properly install your personal car seat using LATCH anchors or seatbelt routing. All seats meet or exceed federal motor vehicle safety standards, and our drivers verify correct installation before every trip.

Can you handle custody exchange transportation?

Yes, Nebo Rides regularly manages custody exchange and visitation transportation for divorced or separated families. Our drivers follow court-ordered schedules precisely and provide timestamped pickup and drop-off confirmations to both parents. We serve as a neutral, professional third party that keeps the child's comfort and safety at the center of every exchange, reducing stress for the entire family.

What happens if my child's schedule changes last minute?

We understand that children's schedules change frequently due to after-school activities, early dismissals, or unexpected events. Semester contract clients can modify pickup and drop-off times with as little as two hours' notice at no additional charge. Our dispatch team is available around the clock to accommodate schedule changes, and your dedicated nanny driver stays flexible to accommodate the real-world unpredictability of family life.
