Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about nanny and minor transportation.

What makes a driver safe for my child?

Our 'Nanny Drivers' undergo enhanced background checks, including Vulnerable Sector clearance, and have clean driving records.

Can I track the ride?

Yes, you will receive a live GPS link the moment the vehicle departs, allowing you to watch the entire journey in real-time.

Do you provide car seats?

We can provide age-appropriate car seats (rear-facing, forward-facing, boosters) upon request, or we can use your personal seat.

Ready to secure your child's commute?

Our family services team is standing by to plan your school run.
