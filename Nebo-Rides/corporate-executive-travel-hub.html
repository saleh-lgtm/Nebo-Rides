Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our corporate executive travel solutions.

Do you integrate with Concur?

Yes. We offer full integration with SAP Concur for booking and expensing. Trip data including pickup and drop-off locations, mileage, wait times, and cost center allocations flows automatically into your Concur profile, eliminating manual expense entry and accelerating month-end reconciliation for your finance team.

Can we set spending limits?

Yes. Corporate accounts can define vehicle classes and spend caps by employee level (e.g., VP gets S-Class, Manager gets E-Class). Our platform enforces these policies at the time of booking, ensuring compliance with your corporate travel policy without requiring manual approval workflows for standard requests.

Do you offer monthly billing?

Yes. Approved corporate accounts receive consolidated monthly invoicing with detailed project coding. Each invoice includes trip-level detail with department allocations, cost center breakdowns, and executive-level spend summaries, making it easy for your accounts payable team to process and reconcile against budget.
