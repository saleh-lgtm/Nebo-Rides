Retail Logistics

Every detail optimized for the perfect shopping experience.

Curbside Wait

Your shopping chauffeur stages in the nearest legal waiting zone, monitored by GPS, and pulls up to the store entrance within minutes of your text message summons. This curbside-on-demand approach means you never stand on a sidewalk holding bags waiting for your car. Our drivers know the staging patterns of every major luxury shopping district, positioning themselves for the fastest possible response time between boutiques.

Secure Storage

Your driver remains with the vehicle at all times, providing continuous security for high-value purchases stored in the trunk or cargo area. Leaving designer bags, jewelry, or electronics in an unattended car is a theft risk that our service completely eliminates. For clients purchasing items worth tens of thousands of dollars on a single shopping trip, this attended vehicle security provides invaluable peace of mind throughout the entire day.

Hands-Free

Simply text your driver after each store visit and they will pull up, take your shopping bags, and organize them securely in the vehicle so you can walk into the next boutique completely unencumbered. This hands-free shopping experience means no juggling bags between stores, no sore arms, and no fatigue that cuts your shopping day short. Our clients consistently report they visit more stores and enjoy the experience significantly more with a dedicated bag-handling chauffeur.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our private shopping chauffeur service.

Does the driver wait right in front?

Your shopping chauffeur stages in the nearest legal waiting zone and pulls up to the curbside within minutes of receiving your text message. Our drivers know the exact staging spots and loading zones for every major luxury shopping district, from Rodeo Drive to Bond Street. You will never stand on a sidewalk holding bags, as the vehicle arrives before you even reach the curb.

Are my purchases safe?

Yes, your driver remains with the vehicle at all times, providing continuous attended security for all bags, boxes, and packages stored in the trunk or cargo area. This eliminates the risk of theft that comes with leaving high-value merchandise in an unattended parked car. For clients purchasing luxury goods worth thousands of dollars, this attended vehicle security is one of the most valued aspects of our shopping chauffeur service.

Can we fit a lot of bags?

For serious shopping trips, we recommend booking a Cadillac Escalade or Mercedes Sprinter to ensure ample cargo space for garment bags, shoe boxes, and oversized purchases. Our dispatchers will help you choose the right vehicle based on your planned itinerary and expected purchase volume. If you end up buying more than expected, we can arrange a second vehicle to be dispatched to your location to handle the overflow.

Can we book a shopping chauffeur for a group trip?

Absolutely. Our Sprinter Limo is perfect for group shopping excursions like birthday celebrations, bachelorette parties, and "girls' day out" experiences. The vehicle accommodates up to 13 guests with generous cargo space for everyone's purchases, plus optional champagne and refreshments. Group shopping trips include the same curbside pickup, bag handling, and attended vehicle security that make our individual shopping chauffeur service exceptional.

Can I change my plans mid-trip?

Yes, our "As-Directed" shopping service gives you complete flexibility to change your itinerary at any moment. Decide to skip a store, add a lunch stop, visit a different mall, or extend your shopping day by several hours. The car is exclusively yours for the duration of the booking, with no restrictions on destination changes or additional stops. This spontaneous flexibility is what separates a private shopping chauffeur from a standard point-to-point car service.
