Trustworthiness

Even for long-term chauffeur assignments spanning months, our safety oversight never rests. Think of us as your outsourced transportation department for the duration of your Texas relocation or extended project. Operational Excellence

Rapid Integration

Your dedicated chauffeur receives a comprehensive briefing on your household or corporate protocols before the first day of the assignment. This includes preferred routes, gate codes, school pickup procedures, dietary preferences for stocked beverages, and communication style expectations. Our rapid integration process ensures your long-term private driver operates as a seamless extension of your daily routine from the very first morning.

Family Logistics

Your long-term chauffeur manages transportation for the entire household, including daily school drop-offs and pickups, medical appointments, grocery runs, and social engagements. Drivers assigned to family accounts are selected for their patience, discretion, and experience with child safety protocols including proper car seat installation. This comprehensive family logistics coverage is especially valuable for relocating families who are still learning the roads and neighborhoods of their new Texas city.

Fleet Rotation

For extended assignments, we manage a proactive vehicle maintenance schedule that includes regular inspections, detailing, and mechanical servicing to keep your dedicated vehicle in showroom condition throughout the engagement. If a vehicle requires service, we provide a seamless same-class replacement so your daily schedule is never disrupted. This fleet rotation protocol ensures the reliability and comfort that multi-week and multi-month chauffeur assignments demand.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our long-term chauffeur assignments in Texas.

What is the minimum duration for a long-term assignment?

We typically consider "long-term" to mean assignments of two weeks or longer, though we offer flexible terms to accommodate varying relocation timelines and project durations. Many of our Texas-based clients start with a one-month trial period and extend as needed, with no penalties for early termination or timeline adjustments. Our most common long-term chauffeur assignments range from three to twelve months for executive relocations.

Will I have the same driver every day?

Yes, driver continuity is our top priority for every long-term assignment. We assign a single primary chauffeur who learns your preferences, routes, and schedule, along with a dedicated backup driver for off-days and holidays. Both drivers receive your specific protocol briefing so the transition on backup days is seamless. This consistency is the defining advantage of a long-term chauffeur assignment over on-demand car services.

Can I travel between cities?

Yes, your dedicated vehicle and driver can take you between Houston, Dallas, San Antonio, Austin, and Fort Worth as needed throughout your assignment. Inter-city travel is included in your long-term chauffeur package with no additional booking required. Many of our relocating executives make regular weekly trips between Texas metros for meetings, and having the same driver who knows the routes and timing makes these journeys productive and stress-free.

What happens if I need to extend or shorten my assignment?

We understand that relocation timelines and project durations shift, which is why our long-term assignments are built with flexible terms. Extensions can be arranged with as little as one week's notice, and early termination carries no penalties beyond the current billing cycle. Your dedicated account manager handles all adjustments so you never have to renegotiate from scratch.

Can my family members use the dedicated driver independently?

Absolutely. Long-term family assignments are designed for shared use across your entire household. Your spouse, children, and other authorized family members can each use the dedicated driver for school runs, medical appointments, errands, and social engagements. We provide a shared scheduling portal so all family members can see availability and book time with the driver directly, keeping everyone's logistics coordinated under one assignment.
