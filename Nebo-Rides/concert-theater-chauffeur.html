Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about concert and theater transportation.

How do I find my driver after the concert?

Your driver will coordinate a specific meeting point away from the main exit crush and communicate via SMS. We send the exact pickup location and driver details 15 minutes before the show ends, so you can walk directly to your waiting vehicle without searching through crowds.

Is alcohol allowed in the vehicle?

Alcohol is generally permitted in partitioned vehicles like limousines and Sprinters, but not in standard sedans or SUVs. This varies by local regulations in each city, and our booking team will confirm the specific policy for your market when you reserve.

Can you make multiple stops for our group?

Yes, we can route multiple pickups and drop-offs to ensure your entire party arrives and returns safely. Our as-directed service allows unlimited stops, so you can collect friends from different locations before the show and drop everyone home individually afterward.

What if the show runs longer than expected?

Your driver remains on standby for the entire duration of the event, whether it runs on time or extends for multiple encores. There is no penalty for shows that run late, and our dispatch team monitors live event updates to keep your chauffeur positioned and ready for whenever you exit.

Can you arrange transportation for a corporate client entertainment evening?

Absolutely. We regularly coordinate multi-vehicle entertainment outings for corporate clients, including separate pickups from hotels, synchronized venue arrivals, and coordinated post-show transfers to restaurants or after-parties. Our corporate accounts team can manage billing, manifest logistics, and VIP coordination for groups of any size.
