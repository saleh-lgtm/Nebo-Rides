Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about manifest management.

How do you group passengers?

We analyze arrival times and group attendees within configurable windows (typically 30 minutes) to maximize shuttle efficiency while minimizing individual wait times. Our algorithm factors in terminal location, customs clearance estimates, and hotel destination clusters to create the most cost-effective groupings. VIP attendees can be assigned dedicated vehicles outside the grouping logic at your direction.

What is the deadline for manifest changes?

We accept manifest changes up to 24 hours prior to the event for optimal vehicle planning and cost efficiency. However, our system is designed for the reality of live events, so we maintain dedicated sweeper vehicles for last-minute additions and cancellations. Changes submitted within the 24-hour window are processed immediately and reflected in driver dispatches in real time.

Is attendee data secure?

Yes, we are fully GDPR compliant and transmit all manifests via AES-256 encrypted channels with role-based access controls. Passenger data is stored on SOC 2 compliant servers and is automatically purged within 72 hours post-event. We can also execute custom data processing agreements (DPAs) for organizations with specific compliance requirements.

What file formats do you accept for manifest uploads?

We accept Excel (.xlsx), CSV, and direct API exports from event platforms including Cvent, Eventbrite, and Bizzabo. Our data team can also work with custom formats from internal HR or registration systems. For recurring events, we set up automated data pipelines that pull attendee lists directly from your platform without manual file transfers.

Can attendees see their specific ride details?

Yes, each attendee receives a personalized mobile link or SMS with their vehicle assignment, driver name, vehicle description, and real-time tracking. This white-label guest experience can be branded with your event or company logo. Attendees can also use the link to communicate directly with their assigned driver if they encounter delays at customs or baggage claim.
