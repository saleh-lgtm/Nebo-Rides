EXPERTISE

PNR Synchronization

We scrutinize every PNR for accuracy and consistency. If the flight segment changes in Sabre or Amadeus, our system detects the modification and automatically updates the corresponding car segment, adjusting pickup times, terminal assignments, and driver positioning without requiring manual intervention from the agent. This automated synchronization eliminates the most common source of ground transportation failures: stale booking data caused by itinerary changes that were never communicated to the car service provider.

Booking Logic

Seamless integration with your existing booking workflow.

PNR Sync

Our system monitors PNR modifications in real-time and automatically updates ground transportation segments when flight times, terminals, or airports change. This automated synchronization eliminates the risk of stale pickup times that lead to missed connections and client complaints. Agents receive confirmation notifications for every automated update, maintaining full visibility without manual intervention.

Instant Confirmations

Every booking receives an immediate confirmation number with vehicle class, chauffeur assignment details, and pickup instructions. Real-time availability checks across our global network ensure that rates displayed in the GDS reflect actual vehicle availability, not aspirational inventory. This accuracy reduces cancellation rates and eliminates the back-and-forth confirmation delays common with other ground transportation providers.

Audit Trails

Every booking action, modification, and cancellation is logged with timestamps, agent identifiers, and change details for complete audit trail documentation. These comprehensive records support agency compliance requirements, dispute resolution, and commission reconciliation processes. Reports can be exported in standard formats compatible with major travel management and accounting platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything travel agents need to know about GDS booking.

Where can I find your GDS codes?

Our GDS clues for Sabre, Amadeus, and Travelport are available in the secure agent portal immediately after completing the registration process. Your dedicated agency representative can also provide the codes directly via email or phone during the onboarding call. We maintain current availability and rate loading across all major GDS platforms to ensure accurate booking information.

When are commissions paid?

We process commission payments monthly via TACS or direct deposit, typically on a net-30 basis following trip completion. Detailed commission statements are available for download through the agent portal, showing individual booking breakdowns, commission rates, and payment totals. For agencies with high booking volumes, we offer accelerated payment schedules and dedicated finance contacts for commission inquiries.

Do you offer net rates?

Yes, authenticated agencies can access net rates to apply their own markup directly within the Nebo Agent Web portal. Net rate access is available for agencies that meet minimum volume thresholds, which are discussed during the onboarding process. This model gives your agency complete control over client-facing pricing while maintaining transparent cost visibility on the supplier side.

Will you ever contact my clients directly?

Never. We operate a strict no-solicitation policy regarding agency-booked clients. All communication about future bookings, promotions, or service offerings is directed exclusively to the booking agent, never to the end traveler. Our drivers present as your agency's partner in the field, and all post-trip follow-up is routed through the agency relationship. Client protection is fundamental to our travel trade partnership model.

Can I book complex multi-leg itineraries through the GDS?

Standard point-to-point transfers and hourly bookings can be completed directly through Sabre and Amadeus. For complex multi-leg itineraries, as-directed service, multi-day engagements, or event logistics that exceed standard GDS segment capabilities, we recommend using the Nebo Agent Web portal or contacting our dedicated agent desk directly. These channels provide the flexibility needed for sophisticated booking requirements that GDS systems were not designed to handle.
