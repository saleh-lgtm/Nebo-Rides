Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our real estate tour services.

How many stops can we make?

Our hourly 'As-Directed' service allows for unlimited stops. We maximize your time to see as many properties as possible.

Can we brand the vehicle?

For developer roadshows or project launches, we can arrange for magnetic signage or vinyl wrapping on Sprinter vans.

What if the tour runs over?

Real estate is unpredictable. If a tour runs long, we automatically extend the booking in 15-minute increments for your convenience.
