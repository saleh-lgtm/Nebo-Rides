Showing Logistics

Every detail optimized for the perfect property tour experience.

Route Optimization

Our drivers logically sequence 5-6 property viewings to minimize drive time and maximize showing opportunities. We factor in traffic patterns, listing agent availability windows, and neighborhood proximity to build the most efficient tour itinerary for your clients.

Mobile Office

The cabin becomes a productive workspace for reviewing comparable sales data, floor plans, and investment analyses between property stops. Our vehicles feature Wi-Fi connectivity, device chargers, and privacy partitions so brokers can have candid strategy discussions with clients in comfort.

Climate Staging

While clients view properties, the vehicle stays running with climate control engaged so they return to a cool or warm cabin regardless of weather conditions. This attention to comfort is especially appreciated during summer heat waves and winter cold snaps, ensuring clients remain refreshed throughout a full day of showings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our real estate tour services.

How many stops can we make?

Our hourly 'As-Directed' service allows for unlimited stops, so you can view as many properties as your schedule permits. We maximize your time by optimizing the route between listings based on geography and appointment windows. Most brokers find they can comfortably tour 8-10 properties in a full-day booking.

Can we brand the vehicle?

For developer roadshows or project launches, we can arrange for magnetic signage or vinyl wrapping on Sprinter vans. This is a popular option for new construction marketing events and open house promotions where brand visibility matters. Contact our events team at least one week in advance for custom branding requests.

What if the tour runs over?

Real estate is unpredictable. If a tour runs long, we automatically extend the booking in 15-minute increments for your convenience. There is no need to call ahead or renegotiate; your driver simply continues service until you are finished. This flexibility is built into our real estate tour pricing so you never feel rushed during a critical showing.

Do you offer recurring weekly rates for agents?

Yes, we offer discounted weekly and monthly packages for real estate professionals who require regular showing transportation. Agents who book a standing weekly reservation receive preferred pricing and guaranteed vehicle availability during peak showing hours. Contact our corporate sales team to discuss a custom rate structure for your brokerage.

Can the driver assist with international clients who need translation?

We maintain a roster of multilingual chauffeurs who can provide basic assistance in Mandarin, Spanish, Arabic, French, and Russian. While our drivers are not certified translators, their language skills can help international property buyers feel more comfortable during tours. Request a multilingual driver when booking to ensure availability for your client's preferred language.
