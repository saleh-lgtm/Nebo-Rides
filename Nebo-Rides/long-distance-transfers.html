Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our long-distance transfer service.

Can we make stops along the way?

Yes, unlike a train or bus, you can stop for food, restrooms, or sightseeing whenever you wish. Simply let your driver know and the route will be adjusted accordingly. Popular stops along the NY-Boston route include New Haven and Hartford for campus visits.

Are there luggage limits?

There are no per-bag fees. You can bring as much luggage as safely fits in the vehicle's cargo area. For larger loads, we can upgrade you to an SUV or Sprinter van at the time of booking to ensure everything travels comfortably alongside you.

How is pricing calculated?

We offer fixed, all-inclusive rates for point-to-point transfers, regardless of the number of passengers (up to vehicle capacity). The quoted price includes fuel, tolls, parking, and driver gratuity, so the amount you see is the amount you pay with no hidden fees.

Is Wi-Fi available during the drive?

Yes, all long-distance vehicles are equipped with mobile Wi-Fi hotspots and USB charging ports. Our Sprinter JetVan options also include power outlets for laptops, making it easy to conduct video calls or complete work during the transfer.

Can I bring my pet on a long-distance transfer?

Absolutely. Our SUVs and Sprinter vans are pet-friendly, and your animal travels in the cabin with you rather than in a cargo hold. We schedule rest stops for pet breaks and provide protective seat covers. Just mention your pet when booking so we can prepare the vehicle accordingly.
