Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our long-distance transfer service.

Can we make stops along the way?

Yes, unlike a train or bus, you can stop for food, restrooms, or sightseeing whenever you wish.

Are there luggage limits?

There are no per-bag fees. You can bring as much luggage as safely fits in the vehicle's cargo area.

How is pricing calculated?

We offer fixed, all-inclusive rates for point-to-point transfers, regardless of the number of passengers (up to vehicle capacity).
