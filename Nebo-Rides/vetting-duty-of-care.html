Safety Protocol

The Safety Protocol: Engineering Confidence

We leave nothing to chance.

EXPERTISE

Audit Trail

We maintain a digital audit trail of every driver's license expiration, insurance policy, vehicle inspection report, and background check renewal date. Our compliance management system runs automated daily scans and instantly suspends any chauffeur or vehicle that falls out of compliance, with zero human override possible. This proactive monitoring means your travelers never ride with a driver whose credentials have lapsed, even by a single day.

TRUSTWORTHINESS

Physical Inspections

We do not rely on self-reported photos or partner attestations. Our field operations team physically inspects every partner fleet vehicle for tire tread depth, brake pad wear, fluid levels, exterior condition, and interior cleanliness before the vehicle enters our active roster. These inspections are repeated quarterly, and any vehicle that fails is immediately removed from service until deficiencies are corrected and re-inspected.

AUTHORITATIVENESS

Data Privacy

Your safety extends beyond the physical vehicle to your digital identity. We protect all Personally Identifiable Information with AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit, meeting or exceeding banking-grade security standards. Our SOC2 Type II compliance is independently audited annually, and we maintain strict data retention policies that automatically purge passenger movement data after the period specified in your corporate agreement.

Safety Logistics

Proactive monitoring to keep every journey compliant.

Continuous Monitoring

Real-time alerts flag any license expiration, insurance lapse, or background check renewal before the deadline arrives, not after. Our automated compliance engine scans all driver and vehicle credentials daily, triggering a 30-day warning, a 7-day final notice, and an automatic suspension at expiration. Corporate travel managers receive a monthly compliance report showing 100% active credential status across their assigned driver pool.

Physical Inspections

Verified vehicle quality checks conducted in person by our field operations team, not through self-reported photos or partner assertions. Inspectors evaluate tire condition, brake performance, fluid levels, cabin cleanliness, and safety equipment presence using a standardized 50-point checklist. Vehicles that do not meet our standards are immediately removed from the active fleet until all deficiencies are corrected and re-verified.

Data Encryption

Banking-grade encryption protects every piece of personal and travel data that flows through our platform, from passenger names and phone numbers to credit card details and route histories. All data is encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit, and access is restricted to authorized personnel through role-based permissions with multi-factor authentication. We never sell or share passenger data with third parties under any circumstances.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our vetting and duty of care standards.

Can I request a female driver?

Yes, we accommodate requests for female chauffeurs for solo female travelers, subject to availability in the specific market. This option is frequently requested by corporate travel programs with duty of care policies for female employees traveling internationally. We recommend requesting a female driver at the time of booking to ensure availability, and our dispatch team will confirm the assignment in advance of your pickup.

What are your hygiene protocols?

We maintain rigorous sanitization standards that evolved from our post-pandemic protocols and have become permanent operating procedure. High-touch surfaces including door handles, armrests, seat belt buckles, climate controls, and window buttons are cleaned with hospital-grade disinfectant between every passenger. Interior air quality is maintained through cabin air filters and ventilation protocols that ensure a fresh, odor-free environment for each ride.

What happens in an emergency?

Drivers are trained in comprehensive emergency response protocols that begin with assessing passenger safety, securing the scene to prevent further harm, and immediately contacting local emergency services. Simultaneously, our 24/7 dispatch center is notified through an automated alert system and begins coordinating a replacement vehicle, notifying your corporate travel manager, and documenting the incident for your records. Every vehicle carries a first aid kit, fire extinguisher, and emergency contact cards.

What background checks do you perform on drivers?

Every chauffeur undergoes a multi-jurisdictional criminal background check covering local, state, and federal databases, plus a motor vehicle records review spanning at least 7 years. In the United States, this includes county courthouse records, national sex offender registry, and OFAC sanctions screening. Internationally, we apply equivalent screening standards using local law enforcement databases and third-party verification services. Background checks are renewed annually, not just at onboarding.

Can you provide compliance documentation for our procurement team?

Yes. We provide comprehensive compliance packets specifically designed for corporate procurement and risk management teams conducting vendor due diligence. Documentation includes certificates of insurance, SOC2 Type II audit reports, background check methodology details, drug screening protocols, defensive driving certification standards, and GDPR/CCPA privacy compliance statements. Our compliance team is available for live Q&A sessions with your legal or risk department as needed.
