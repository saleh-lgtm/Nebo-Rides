Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our centralized billing and dispatch services.

How does centralized billing benefit my company?

Centralized billing reduces administrative workload by consolidating all company-wide travel into a single invoice, providing better financial visibility and easier expense tracking. No more chasing individual receipts from multiple vendors across different countries.

Can I see my chauffeurs' locations in real-time?

Yes. Our centralized dispatch portal provides real-time GPS tracking for all active reservations, giving you total visibility for security and coordination. This is essential for "Duty of Care" compliance and travel manager oversight.

How accurate is the invoicing for global trips?

Every trip is logged in our unified database. We provide 100% transparent invoicing with detailed trip data (time, route, vehicle) regardless of which city the service was provided in. All tolls, parking, and gratuities are line-itemed for complete audit readiness.

What billing tiers are available?

We offer flexible billing configurations: individual ride billing for personal expense tracking, department-level accounts for cost center allocation, or a single master corporate invoice for simplified AP processing. Your account manager can configure the optimal structure for your organization.

Do you integrate with corporate expense platforms?

Yes. We offer automated receipt integration with major corporate expense platforms including Concur, Expensify, and others. This reduces employee admin time and ensures all travel expenses flow directly into your existing financial systems.
