Expertise

Facility Knowledge

We know the specific drop-off loops, patient entrances, and valet protocols for major medical centers including Mayo Clinic, Cleveland Clinic, MD Anderson, and Harley Street specialist clinics. Our drivers navigate complex hospital campuses confidently, delivering you to the correct building entrance rather than leaving you to find your way across a sprawling medical campus. This facility-specific knowledge is built from years of providing medical appointment chauffeur services and is continuously updated as hospitals modify their patient access points.

Trustworthiness

Sign-Out Responsible

Many clinics and surgical centers require a "Responsible Adult" to sign patients out after sedation or anesthesia, and our vetted chauffeurs can fulfill this requirement subject to your specific clinic's policies. This eliminates the need to ask friends or family members to take time off work for your procedure. Our drivers carry identification, are background-checked to healthcare facility standards, and understand the discharge process, ensuring a smooth handoff from nursing staff to vehicle without delay or confusion.

Authoritativeness

Privacy

Medical visits are deeply personal, and our chauffeurs adhere to strict confidentiality protocols regarding your destinations, appointment schedules, and medical facilities. Drivers never discuss client itineraries with anyone outside our operations team, and no medical destination information appears on shared ride logs or billing statements beyond generic trip details. This commitment to medical transport privacy is why patients undergoing sensitive treatments and procedures trust Nebo Rides over rideshare alternatives where driver discretion cannot be guaranteed.

Care Logistics

Every detail designed to support your medical journey.

Door-to-Door Assist

Our medical appointment chauffeurs provide physical door-to-door escort assistance, offering a steady arm from your front door to the vehicle and from the vehicle to the clinic entrance. This is especially important for post-surgical patients, elderly passengers, and anyone experiencing mobility limitations or post-sedation grogginess. Drivers are trained to assist with walkers, crutches, and folding wheelchairs, ensuring safe transitions at every step of the journey.

Wait & Return

Your chauffeur waits on-site at the medical facility for the entire duration of your appointment or procedure, ready to drive you home the moment you are discharged. There is no need to call for a pickup or wait for a rideshare to arrive while you are groggy, in pain, or unable to see clearly after eye dilation. We bill only for the actual wait time, so you are never penalized for procedures that run longer than expected.

Facility Access

Our drivers maintain detailed knowledge of patient drop-off loops, parking protocols, and building entrances for major medical centers and specialist clinics across our service areas. They know which entrances are closest to specific departments, where valet services are available, and how to navigate the often confusing layouts of large hospital campuses. This facility-specific expertise ensures you arrive at the correct door on time, reducing stress on what is already an anxious day.

Medical Transportation Programs for Organizations

Concierge medical practices, family offices, and corporate wellness programs partner with Nebo Rides to provide reliable, dignified medical appointment transportation for their patients, principals, and employees. Our medical chauffeur service ensures that individuals undergoing procedures, treatments, or specialist consultations receive door-to-door care that exceeds what standard non-emergency medical transport (NEMT) providers offer.

Centralized Billing

Medical practices and family offices receive a single monthly invoice covering all patient or principal medical transport rides, with HIPAA-conscious billing that lists trip details without disclosing medical destinations or procedure types. This centralized billing approach simplifies expense management for concierge practices serving multiple patients and family offices managing healthcare logistics for HNW individuals.

Priority Dispatch

Medical account holders receive priority vehicle assignment to guarantee on-time arrivals for time-sensitive surgery slots, treatment windows, and specialist consultations. Our dispatch team understands that missing a surgical appointment can mean weeks of rescheduling, which is why we assign dedicated vehicles with buffer time and backup driver coverage for every medical transport booking.

Duty of Care

Every medical appointment chauffeur is background-checked, trained in patient assistance techniques, and operates a hospital-grade sanitized vehicle. Our duty-of-care framework includes door-to-door escort assistance, on-site wait-and-return protocols, and the ability to serve as a Responsible Adult for post-sedation discharge, giving healthcare providers and family offices confidence that their patients are in safe, caring hands throughout the journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our medical transportation service.

Can the driver sign me out after sedation?

Yes, our vetted chauffeurs can act as a Responsible Adult for post-sedation and post-anesthesia discharge, subject to your specific clinic's policies and requirements. We recommend confirming this arrangement with your medical facility in advance, as requirements vary between institutions. Many of our clients use this service for procedures such as colonoscopies, wisdom tooth extractions, Lasik eye surgery, and outpatient surgeries where driving yourself home is medically prohibited.

Do you accommodate wheelchairs?

Our luxury SUVs can accommodate folding manual wheelchairs in the trunk, and drivers are trained to assist with loading and unloading. Please note that we do not currently operate vehicles equipped with hydraulic lifts for powered electric wheelchairs. If you have specific mobility equipment requirements, please mention them when booking so we can assign the most appropriate vehicle and confirm compatibility in advance.

What if the doctor runs late?

Our drivers will wait on-site for the entire duration of your appointment, regardless of how long the delay. We bill for actual time used rather than charging a flat rate, so you are never penalized for a doctor running behind schedule. Your chauffeur remains parked and ready at the facility so the moment you are discharged, your ride home is immediately available with no rebooking or surge pricing required.

Are your vehicles sanitized between rides?

Yes, every vehicle assigned to medical appointment transport is sanitized to hospital-grade standards before each trip using EPA-approved disinfectants on all high-touch surfaces including door handles, armrests, seat belts, and climate controls. This sanitization protocol is especially important for immune-compromised patients undergoing chemotherapy, dialysis, or post-transplant care who cannot risk exposure to pathogens in shared vehicles. We provide sanitization certificates upon request for patients with specific clinical requirements.

Can I set up recurring weekly rides for ongoing treatments?

Absolutely. Many of our medical transport clients set up recurring weekly or bi-weekly ride schedules for ongoing treatments such as chemotherapy, dialysis, physical therapy, and specialist consultations. We assign the same driver to your recurring schedule whenever possible, building a comfortable rapport that makes the experience less stressful over time. Recurring rides are billed at preferred rates and can be adjusted or paused at any time as your treatment plan evolves.
