Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our medical transportation service.

Can the driver sign me out after sedation?

Yes, our vetted drivers can act as a 'Responsible Adult' for discharge, subject to your clinic's specific policies.

Do you accommodate wheelchairs?

Our SUVs can accommodate folding manual wheelchairs. Please note we do not have hydraulic lifts for electric wheelchairs.

What if the doctor runs late?

Our drivers will wait. We bill for the actual time used, so you never have to worry about being stranded.

Need medical transport?

Our caring team is ready to coordinate your medical transportation.
