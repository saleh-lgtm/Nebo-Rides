Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our luxury SUV chauffeur service.

What is the luggage capacity of an SUV?

An SUV like the Cadillac Escalade ESV can hold up to 6 large checked suitcases along with carry-ons in the rear cargo area. The extended wheelbase model provides significantly more trunk space than a standard SUV, making it ideal for airport transfers where multiple passengers are traveling with full-size luggage. If you need additional capacity, we can arrange a second vehicle or a luggage trailer for oversized equipment.

Are SUVs suitable for families requiring car seats?

Yes, SUVs are ideal for families as they have LATCH systems and ample space for multiple car seats without crowding adult passengers. We carry forward-facing, rear-facing, and booster seat options that meet federal safety standards. Simply provide the ages and weights of your children at booking, and the correct seats will be professionally installed before your pickup.

Can an SUV transport skis or golf clubs?

Absolutely. The extended cargo space of an Escalade ESV or Suburban easily accommodates oversized items like ski bags, golf clubs, snowboards, and fishing rod cases. Our drivers are experienced in safely loading and securing sporting equipment to prevent shifting during transit. For mountain resort transfers, we recommend the SUV class specifically for its all-weather 4WD capability on snow-covered roads.

What is the difference between an Escalade and a Suburban?

The Cadillac Escalade ESV is the premium flagship with upgraded leather, a more refined suspension, and a stronger brand presence, making it the preferred choice for executive arrivals and VIP transport. The Chevrolet Suburban offers the same platform reliability and interior space at a slightly lower price point, making it excellent for corporate team transport and event logistics. Both vehicles seat up to 6 passengers comfortably with full luggage capacity.

Can I book multiple SUVs for a corporate roadshow?

Yes, multi-vehicle SUV bookings are one of our core competencies. We regularly coordinate fleets of 3 to 15 Escalades for investor roadshows, board retreats, and multi-city deal tours. Our corporate team assigns a dedicated coordinator who manages vehicle staging, driver briefings, and real-time schedule adjustments across all vehicles for the duration of your event.
