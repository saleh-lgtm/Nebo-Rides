Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Board meeting transportation.

Can you accommodate last-minute schedule changes?

Yes, our dispatch team is available 24/7 to handle schedule shifts, often waiving fees for VIP accounts. Board meeting schedules are inherently fluid, and our operations team monitors every active assignment in real-time to ensure vehicles are repositioned instantly when plans change.

Do you work with personal security teams?

Absolutely. We coordinate directly with personal security details, including providing follow cars and vetted drivers who have completed executive protection awareness training. Our dispatch team shares live vehicle tracking data with your security coordinator for full situational awareness throughout the event.

Can billing be split by department?

Yes, we can code invoices to specific cost centers or departments to simplify your corporate accounting. Whether you need separate line items for the audit committee, compensation committee, or full Board sessions, our billing system accommodates multi-level cost allocation with detailed trip-by-trip breakdowns.

How do you handle multi-city Board meetings?

For governance events spanning multiple cities, we assign a single project manager who coordinates ground transportation across all locations. Whether your Board convenes in New York on Monday and London on Wednesday, the same PM manages the manifest, ensuring consistent service standards, unified billing, and seamless handoffs between our local teams at each destination.

What happens if a Director's flight is delayed or canceled?

Our operations team monitors every inbound flight on the Board manifest using real-time aviation tracking systems. When a delay or cancellation is detected, we automatically adjust the pickup schedule, notify your coordinator, and reposition vehicles accordingly. There are no additional charges for flight-related timing changes on Board meeting accounts.
