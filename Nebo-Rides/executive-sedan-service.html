Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our executive sedan service.

How many passengers can an executive sedan hold?

Executive sedans like the Mercedes S-Class comfortably seat up to 3 passengers, though we recommend 2 for the optimal rear-cabin experience with full legroom and privacy. For groups of 3 or more executives, we recommend pairing an S-Class with a second sedan or upgrading to an executive SUV for additional space.

How much luggage fits in a sedan?

The trunk of a Mercedes S-Class or BMW 7-Series typically accommodates 2 large checked suitcases plus a carry-on bag. For executives traveling with golf clubs, oversized equipment, or more than 2 bags, we recommend upgrading to a Cadillac Escalade or Lincoln Navigator executive SUV for ample luggage capacity.

Can I request a specific vehicle model?

While we cannot guarantee a specific color or interior trim, we guarantee the vehicle class such as S-Class, 7-Series, or equivalent luxury sedan with identical amenity standards. Corporate account holders can set model preferences in their profile, and our dispatch team prioritizes those preferences when allocating vehicles from our fleet.

Is Wi-Fi available in executive sedans?

Yes, all executive sedans in our fleet are equipped with complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi, USB charging ports, and power outlets so you can take calls, respond to emails, or prepare presentations during transit. The Mercedes S-Class and BMW 7-Series rear cabins are engineered as true mobile offices with noise isolation and ambient lighting for productive work environments.

Do you offer executive sedan service internationally?

Yes, Nebo Rides provides executive sedan service in over 500 cities worldwide through our vetted global affiliate network. Whether you need an S-Class in Frankfurt, a BMW 7-Series in Tokyo, or a luxury sedan in Singapore, we guarantee consistent vehicle standards, centralized billing, and 24/7 US-based support across every international market.
