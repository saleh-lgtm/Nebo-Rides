Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our technology and app integration.

Can I book for someone else?

Yes, our 'Book for Guest' feature allows you to arrange rides for colleagues or clients, with updates sent directly to them.

How do I change a ride?

You can modify or cancel bookings instantly via the mobile app or web portal without needing to call dispatch.

Does the app track flights?

Absolutely. Enter your flight number during booking, and our system automatically tracks status and adjusts pickup times for delays.
