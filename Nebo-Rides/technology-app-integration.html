The Tech Protocol: Engineering Uptime

Technology should serve the user, not the other way around.

EXPERTISE

Latency

Our global dispatch grid updates every 5 seconds, ensuring the location you see on the map is the reality on the ground. This near-real-time accuracy is powered by redundant GPS feeds that cross-reference cellular triangulation with satellite positioning. The result is sub-10-meter accuracy for ETAs, giving travel managers and passengers confidence that arrival times are reliable, not estimates.

TRUSTWORTHINESS

Data Security

We are SOC2 Type II compliant, meaning our security controls are independently audited on an ongoing basis, not just at a single point in time. Your passenger manifests, credit card information, and movement data are encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.3 protocols. We never sell or share your data with third parties, and all PII is automatically purged after the retention period specified in your corporate agreement.

AUTHORITATIVENESS

API First

Large enterprise? Connect your internal travel tool, TMC platform, or corporate intranet directly to our dispatch system via our well-documented REST API. Our integration team provides sandbox environments, webhook support, and dedicated engineering resources to ensure a seamless connection. Current API partners include major travel management companies, expense platforms, and Fortune 500 internal booking systems.

Digital Logistics

Seamless data flow across every touchpoint.

Real-Time Sync

Instant data flow between the mobile app, web portal, and dispatch ensures every booking change is reflected across all platforms within seconds. Whether your EA modifies a pickup time on the web booker or a traveler updates the destination in the app, all stakeholders see the same information in real time. This eliminates the miscommunication that plagues phone-based booking workflows.

Expense Integration

Automated receipt forwarding to Concur, Expensify, Deel, and other major expense platforms means zero manual data entry for your travelers. Each completed ride generates a detailed digital receipt that flows directly into the correct expense report with pre-populated GL codes and cost center tags. Your finance team gets clean, auditable data without chasing employees for missing receipts at month-end.

Proactive Alerts

SMS and push notifications keep every stakeholder informed with automatic status updates at each critical milestone: Chauffeur En Route, Chauffeur On Location, Passenger On Board, and Trip Completed. Notifications can be configured per user role, so the EA receives all alerts while the traveler gets only the essentials. Custom webhook integrations allow these events to trigger actions in your own systems, such as updating a CRM record or notifying a building concierge.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our technology and app integration.

Can I book for someone else?

Yes, our 'Book for Guest' feature allows executive assistants, office managers, and travel coordinators to arrange rides for colleagues or clients without requiring them to have a Nebo Rides account. The guest receives SMS and email notifications with driver details, vehicle information, and a live tracking link. You maintain full visibility and control over the booking from your admin portal.

How do I change a ride?

You can modify or cancel bookings instantly via the mobile app or web portal without needing to call dispatch. Changes to pickup time, location, vehicle type, or passenger details are reflected in real time across all systems and immediately communicated to the assigned chauffeur. Cancellation policies vary by booking type, but modifications are always free up to 1 hour before the scheduled pickup.

Does the app track flights?

Absolutely. Enter your flight number during booking, and our system automatically tracks real-time flight status including delays, gate changes, and early arrivals. The chauffeur's pickup time adjusts dynamically so they are waiting curbside when you clear customs or baggage claim. This integration works for both domestic and international flights across all major airports in our network.

Can the platform integrate with our existing TMC or travel tool?

Yes. Our REST API is designed for enterprise integration with travel management companies, internal booking tools, and corporate intranets. We provide comprehensive documentation, sandbox testing environments, and dedicated integration engineering support to ensure a seamless connection. Current partners include major TMCs, HR platforms, and Fortune 500 proprietary travel systems.

Is the platform available in all 500+ cities?

Yes. One app, one login, one experience across every city in our global network. Whether you are booking a sedan in London, an SUV in Dubai, or a Sprinter in New York, the same mobile app and web portal handle everything. There is no need to download local apps or create separate accounts when traveling internationally.
